# Ink Paper

**A tactile, highly customizable theme blending the warmth of physical paper with modern workspace layouts, powered by the soothing Flexoki color palette.**

Ink Paper was built from the ground up to provide a distraction-free, visually unified writing experience. It strips away the heavy, opaque backgrounds of the default Obsidian UI, allowing a custom paper texture to flow seamlessly through your panes, sidebars, and active tabs.

## 🌟 Core Features

- **Warm, Readable Typography:** Built natively around the **Flexoki** color system to reduce eye strain in both Light and Dark modes.
    
- **Tactile UI Elements:** Beautifully styled blockquotes with interactive borders, seamless callouts, and extensive support for alternate custom checkboxes (including progress, info, question, and cancellation markers).
    
- **Translucent Architecture:** Sidebars, title bars, and file explorers have been rebuilt to be completely transparent, allowing your chosen background texture to serve as the unified canvas for your entire vault.
    

## 🎨 The Paper Engine (V3)

Most textured themes struggle with UI bleeding and overlapping pseudo-elements. Ink Paper introduces a highly robust, native CSS background engine:

- **True Compositing:** Mix your Base Background Color, a Texture Image URL, and standard CSS Blend Modes (Multiply, Overlay, Color-Burn, etc.) directly in your settings.
    
- **The "Swatch Shield":** A custom UI fix built into the theme that permanently stops background textures from turning your color picker menus transparent.
    
- **Independent Controls:** Effortlessly adjust the opacity of both your base color and your texture grain without writing a single line of CSS.
    

## 🎛️ Modular Workspace Layouts

Whether you prefer a flush, traditional text editor or a modern, separated workspace, Ink Paper adapts to your workflow.

- **Card Layout Mode:** Instantly transform your editor, left panel, and right panel into floating "cards" with adjustable gaps and border radiuses.
    
- **Independent Panel Colors:** Want a dark sidebar with a light editor? You can independently toggle and colorize the Left Panel, Middle Panel, Right Panel, and Vault Profile areas.
    
- **Custom Borders:** Add solid, dashed, or dotted borders to your workspace panes with custom thickness and accent colors.

## ⚙️ Requirements & Customization

To unlock the full potential of Ink Paper, the **Style Settings** community plugin is highly recommended.

Through the Style Settings menu, you can instantly toggle:

- Light and Dark mode base colors, texture URLs, and blend modes.
    
- Card layouts vs. Default flush layouts.
    
- Active tab indicator colors and styling.
    
- Panel border visibility and thickness.
    

### 📥 Installation

1. Open Obsidian and navigate to **Settings > Appearance**.
    
2. Click **Manage** under Themes.
    
3. Search for **Ink Paper** and click **Install and Use**.
    
4. Download the **Style Settings** plugin from the Community Plugins tab to customize your textures and layouts!
